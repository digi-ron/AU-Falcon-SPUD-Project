Notes

Due to the limited details included in the publicly available information, note the following when reading through this data:

  • This information was last checked during December 2025
  • This list is inaccurate for discerning specific trim models of Falcon e.g. S, SR, Forte, XR*. This is due to them all sharing the Falcon model name. Fairmont, Fairlane, LTD, and Futura are exempt from this issue.
  • This doesn't necessarily denote how many of each model are left, but rather how many are actively registered to be driven on Australian or New Zealand roads
  • (Australian Data only) Due to overlapping manufacture months of the EF and BA model Falcons and insufficient information, numbers for 1998 and 2002 models are likely inaccurate. In an attempt for accuracy, these numbers are calculated based on the months of the year, e.g. 1998 numbers are calculated to 25% as the AU was only sold from September to December that year (1/4 of the months)
  • New Zealand data is much more likely to be accurate, but is based heavily on engine number calculation. As a result there is likely a small but present margin of error
  • New Zealand data has been modified from its source for this page, both to make it easier to implement in this page, and because the source file was 1.5GB
